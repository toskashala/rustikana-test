Discover Our Story

Rock Kafana Rustikana is a family-owned restaurant that makes the crossover from the traditional Macedonian kafana (tavern) with all the great local food to a bar or a pub with all the cool drinks and rock and roll music. We aim to be as cozy as your living room and entertain you as your favorite night-out place. Never ever compromise on quality, and provide service as you are a guest at our home.
